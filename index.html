Bedrock

Bedrock is bringing tech leaders from all over the world to discuss all the challenges about being a tech leader in this era.

The foundation of the tech team, the bedrock, is the people who manage their resources internally and champion the team externally.

Some topics that seem natural to cover: Recruiting talent, enabling talent, being the interface to the rest of the company, philosophies around involving the team in non-tech issues, software development, remote working, etc..
